Complete the Candidate Kit (online application + essays)Summer/Early Fall

Request official high school transcripts sent to AFAFall

✓
Monitor candidate portal every 2 weeks for outstanding items and rolling deadlinesOngoing
AFA assigns rolling deadlines specific to your file. Missing a portal deadline is the most common avoidable mistake that ends candidacies.
